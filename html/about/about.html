What is a Hawker Centre?

Hawker centres are an essential part of Singapore’s culture and daily life. They are open-air food complexes that house many individual food stalls, each specialising in different cuisines such as Chinese, Malay, Indian, and other local favourites.

These centres provide affordable, high-quality meals to the public and serve as important social spaces where people from all walks of life gather to eat, work, and connect.

About This Platform

This Hawker Centre Management System is designed to make discovering hawker centres and stalls easier. Users can explore available centres, browse stalls, and manage their orders through a single, organised platform.

Our goal is to modernise the hawker experience while preserving the tradition and accessibility that make hawker centres unique.
