Welcome to HawkerRadar!

What are we?

HawkerRadar is your go-to platform for discovering the best hawker food across Singapore. From west to east, north to central, we’ve got every corner covered. Built by food lovers for food lovers.

We help you find trusted stalls, crowd favourites, and hidden gems, all in one simple and easy-to-use place.

Our goal

Our mission is to make choosing great hawker food effortless. Whether you’re nearby, exploring a new neighbourhood, or just craving something specific, HawkerRadar points you in the right direction.

We want to support local hawkers while giving users reliable, community-driven recommendations they can trust.

Don’t fret — we ensure the highest standards

Carefully curated stalls with verified information so you always know what you’re getting.

Honest reviews given by real people, we say 'NO' to ai-generated biasness.

Consistent quality checks to ensure stalls meet expectations over time.

Don’t stress lah, makan shiok, HawkerRadar got you.
