Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions.

What is this system about?

This system helps users browse hawker centres and order food.

Do I need an account to order?

You can order as a guest, but registered users get extra features.

Can I cancel an order?

Orders can only be cancelled before the vendor accepts them.

How do I create an account?

Click on the Register button and fill in your details.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Use the “Forgot Password” link on the login page to reset it.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can update your email in the account settings.

What services are available?

Food ordering, stall reviews, and order tracking.

Are there promotions or discounts?

Yes, vendors may run discounts; check the promotions section.

Can I like or review a stall?

Yes, registered users can rate and submit feedback for stalls.

The website is not loading. What should I do?

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

Why are images not showing?

Check your internet connection and browser settings.

Who can I contact for technical support?

Use the contact form or email us at info@example.com.
